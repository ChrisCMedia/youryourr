Barrier-Free Senior Living in Biesenthal

The Senior Living Biesenthal project stands for innovative living solutions that combine comfort, barrier-free living, and comprehensive care in a natural setting. With 69 modern serviced apartments, this project offers tailored solutions for age-appropriate living, complemented by day care and outpatient care services.

Challenge

Senior Living Tailored to Needs in Barnim Nature Park

Demographic change presents new challenges to society: the proportion of elderly people is steadily increasing, significantly raising the demand for age-appropriate living arrangements. The challenge is to combine comfortable, barrier-free living with professional care while addressing the individual needs of this growing population. The Senior Living Biesenthal project responds specifically to this trend and offers a contemporary solution in an idyllic setting.
