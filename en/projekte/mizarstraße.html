Success Story

Successful Placement at Mizar Gate Office

Completed: 26/04/2023

We are pleased to announce our latest success in the leasing of a recently completed project! The Mizar Gate Office (MGO) in Schönefeld, originally planned as an office complex, has been partially repurposed and is now being leased by the renowned hotel group Novum Hospitality.

A hotel with 150 rooms as well as 175 serviced apartments will be created, with Novum occupying two of the three individual buildings with a total of 12,405 sqm. Building A will house the "The niu Pax" hotel, while building C will feature apartments of the long-stay brand "Acora Living the City". The fit-out is scheduled for completion in nine to twelve months, with opening planned for summer 2024.

With a total rental area of 19,200 sqm (including Building B), the complex is almost fully let, which is a major success for our company and all parties involved. The third building (B) is currently used as office space and is 70% leased. Negotiations with potential additional tenants are ongoing.

We would like to thank DIEAG for the successful cooperation and the trust placed in our capabilities. Our team at Your Times Real Estate worked hard to bring this transaction to a successful conclusion, and we are proud of the results achieved.

This project demonstrates our ability to successfully broker real estate and to quickly adapt to changing market conditions. We look forward to further successes in the future!
